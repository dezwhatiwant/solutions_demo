The Scenario
Hoth Industries manufactures air handling and cooling products for data centers. You just
completed a discovery call with their team. They urgently need help and are asking for
many things.
Your job:
1. “Listen” to what they said (read transcript below)
2. Decide what they need
3. Build a working prototype that solves a real problem
4. Demo it like you're presenting back to them for feedback
